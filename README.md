# laws-privacy-policy# Privacy Policy

**App name:** Merit Utility App  
**Last updated:** April 28, 2026

Merit Utility App is used to support utility operations such as customer/house lookup, meter reading, invoice viewing, and related operational workflows. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and how users can contact us.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect or process the following information when you use it:

- **Account and login information**, such as username, email, user ID, session ID, and authentication status.
- **Operational utility data**, such as house number, resident/customer name, meter number, zone, previous reading, current reading, units consumed, reading date, invoice data, and payment or billing-related information returned from the connected backend system.
- **Collector or employee information**, such as the logged-in user name, role, employee/user ID, and assigned service area where applicable.
- **Device and app data**, such as app version, device connection status, printer settings, and diagnostic information needed to run the app.
- **Local app data**, such as cached login/session values, selected settings, cached meter/customer data, and other information stored on the device to support app functionality.

## How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

- Authenticate users and maintain secure sessions.
- Retrieve houses, customers, meters, readings, invoices, and related records from the backend system.
- Submit meter readings and related updates to the backend.
- Calculate or display units, prices, invoices, and reading history.
- Support printing, sharing, or opening generated documents where those features are used.
- Diagnose errors, improve reliability, and support authorized users.
- Comply with operational, legal, accounting, or audit requirements where applicable.

## Backend System and Data Storage

Merit Utility App connects to an organization-managed backend system, including Odoo-based services. Data entered in the app may be transmitted to and stored in that backend system.

Some data may also be stored locally on the device using secure storage or app preferences. This local storage is used for app operation, such as maintaining session state, user settings, and cached records. Users should keep their device secure and avoid sharing their login credentials.

## Permissions

Depending on the features used, the app may request permissions such as:

- **Internet/network access** to connect to the backend system.
- **Bluetooth or nearby-device access** for printer connectivity where supported.
- **File or storage access** to create, open, pick, or share documents such as invoices or PDFs where supported by the operating system.
- **Camera or media access** only if the app feature in use requires choosing or capturing files/images.

The app only uses these permissions to provide the requested feature.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with:

- The organization that manages the utility service and backend system.
- Authorized administrators, employees, or service providers who support the app and system.
- Legal, regulatory, accounting, or audit parties when required by law or organizational policy.

## Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. Users should protect their device, keep credentials confidential, and report suspected unauthorized access promptly.

## Data Retention

Operational records such as customer details, readings, invoices, and audit history are generally retained in the backend system according to the organization’s retention, accounting, and legal requirements. Local app data may remain on the device until the user signs out, clears app data, or uninstalls the app.

## User Choices and Rights

Depending on applicable law and organizational policy, users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information. Some operational records may need to be retained for legal, billing, audit, or accounting reasons.

To request assistance, contact the support team or your organization’s administrator.

## Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for authorized organizational or utility-service use and is not directed to children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date above.
